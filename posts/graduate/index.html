Big Data for Healthcare Analytics

MIMIC3 readmission prediction

This class introduced many big data analytics method as applied to electronics health records processing. For the final thesis paper, I researched on the topic of readmission to hospital after discharge. I harvested the readmission labels from the MIMIC-3 database and then prepared features from patients’ vitals, labs, and demographics. The readmission label criterion looks for people who came back to hospital within 30 days, essentially discharged while their inpatient health progression would indicate for longer stay. The RNN (recurrent neural network) model with GRU in was built in pytorch, and then again in tensorflow. It achieved an AUROC of 71%. Though being a blackbox, it was not interpretable. From this project, I learned the importance of interpretability to make the model useful for advancing the understanding of healthcare science.
